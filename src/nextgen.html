This year we're bring 100 of the Next Generation of hackers together in Durham creat, innovate and push technology to new limits.

We'll be providing mentors, APIs, hardware and other cool tech resources to empower them to create great projects in just 24 hours.

The event is only open to children who will be at least 13 and under 18 for the duration of the event.
